# University Student FAQ

## Course Registration
Students must register for courses through the online portal before the deadline. Registration opens two weeks before the semester starts. Late registration incurs a penalty fee.

## Library Services
The university library offers book borrowing, study rooms, online databases, and research assistance. Students can borrow up to 10 books for 3 weeks.

## Examination Rules
Exams are held twice a year. Students must present valid ID. No electronic devices are allowed. Cheating results in immediate failure.

## Fee Payment
Fees must be paid by the 15th of each month. Payment can be made via bank transfer or online payment portal. Late payment attracts a 10% penalty.

## Hostel Application
Hostel applications open in July. Students must submit medical records and pay a deposit. Rooms are allocated on first-come, first-served basis.

## ICT Support
ICT support is available Monday-Friday, 8am-5pm. Students can get help with Wi-Fi, email, and software issues.

## Academic Calendar
First Semester: August - December
Second Semester: January - May
Summer Semester: June - July
